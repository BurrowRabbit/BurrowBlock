Overview

Welcome to BurrowBlock, your digital carrot on a stick! ü•ï This Chrome extension is here to save you from the endless warren of internet distractions. It's like having a stern (but loving) rabbit guardian who gently nudges you away from those tempting online burrows and towards greener, more productive pastures.

Features

  • Pre-loaded with popular rabbit holes like YouTube, Reddit, Netflix, and more.
  • Customizable block list - because one bunny's distraction is another's research.
  • Redirect feature - hop away from procrastination and into productivity!
  • Limited disabling - only three "cheat hops" per day.
  • Blocks re-enable automatically after 45 minutes - in case you fall down a rabbit hole.
  • If you end your break early and Enable manually, your break time is lost. Plan wisely!
  • User-friendly interface - no advanced burrow-engineering required.

Installation Guide (Load Unpacked)

  1. Download and Unzip the Extension
    • Download BurrowBlock.zip from here .
    • Right-click on the downloaded file and select "Extract All..." to unzip it.
  2. Open Chrome and Access the Extensions Page
    • Open Google Chrome.
    • In the address bar, type chrome://extensions/ and press Enter.
  3. Enable Developer Mode
    • On the Extensions page, toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top right corner.
  4. Load the Unzipped Extension
    • Click the "Load unpacked" button in the top left.
    • Select the unzipped BurrowBlock folder and click "Select Folder."
    • BurrowBlock should now appear in your list of extensions.
  5. Start Blocking Distractions!
    • Click the BurrowBlock icon in your Chrome toolbar to access the extension's popup and customize your settings.

How to Use BurrowBlock

  1. Enable/Disable the Extension
    • Use the "Enable/Disable" button in the popup to toggle the extension on or off.
    • Note: Disabling requires a password and is limited to three times per day.
  2. Customize Blocked Sites
    • Click the "Customize" button in the popup to access the settings page.
    • Add or remove sites from the block list and choose where to redirect when a blocked site is accessed.
    • Save your settings to apply the changes.
  3. Redirect Destination
    • Choose from a list of predefined productive websites or enter a custom redirect URL.

FAQs

Q: How do I reset the block list or redirect URL?

A: Access the settings through the "Customize" button in the popup, make your changes, and click "Save."

Q: I've reached my disable limit for the day. What can I do?

A: The extension will automatically re-enable after 45 minutes, or you can wait until the next day to disable again.

Q: Can I add custom websites to the block list?

A: Not yet, but I'm working on it!.

Q: What happens if I try to unblock a site within the 5-minute window?

A: You'll receive a notification letting you know that the site cannot be unblocked until the time window has passed.
